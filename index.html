    Story of us

    My family and I renovated this restaurant in 2002 after we had been gathering money for years. There is rich history in all of the surroundings of the restaurant since it used to be an old fast food place located in downtown New York where people came by and ordered healthy, delicious food for low prices. We try to continue that legacy by providing the most luscious, savory, and gorgeous food to all of our customers but as a luxurious, affordable alternative to typical high-end restaurants. We believe that food can be art, especially with virtuoso chefs who are masters of finesse.
